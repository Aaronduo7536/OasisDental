Quick Guide for Office Staff

  • Proposed Treatment: Type the exact procedure(s) into the text box.
  • Values & Sliders: You can type exact numbers into the boxes or drag the sliders.
  • Additional Discounts: Use the dropdown to apply a flat amount ($) or a percentage (%) as a manual courtesy before calculation.
  • Insurance & Patient Portion: Entering any insurance will automatically reveal the Patient Portion slider. These two are linked — adjusting one will automatically recalculate the other.
  • Pay-In-Full Rule: If estimated insurance is applied, the 10% Pay-In-Full discount is automatically removed.
  • Payment Schedule: Use the dropdown next to the term length to switch between Months, Bi-Weekly, or Per Visit payments.
  • Interest: The "Total Interest Paid" line stays hidden unless the Interest Rate is set above 0%.
  • Printing vs Saving: Print Estimate gives a clean copy to the patient (instructions hidden). Save as PDF saves the full view for records.
